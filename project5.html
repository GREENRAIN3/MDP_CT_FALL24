My concept began with a pixelated raindrop scene and gradually explored the following aspects: adjusting the shape, size, and density of raindrops to create a sense of visual depth; adding dynamic effects such as wind direction, raindrop trajectories, and trails; enhancing visual contrast through color gradients, brightness adjustments, and highlights; overlaying dynamic ripple distortions and color blending to simulate natural wave motion; experimenting with the addition and removal of dynamic background effects to explore the balance between static and dynamic visuals; and finally, experimenting with various expressive visual techniques, including random color distribution, multi-layered blending effects, and artistic textures, transforming the scene from a simple simulation into more complex and abstract forms.
